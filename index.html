About Us

Our Love

Our story began 2 years ago. We met in Bratislava and couldn't stop talking. We chatted for 8 hours on our first date! The longest first date in the history of dating, probably. It was like catching up with someone you had known for many years. We met our families and friends very early on, and our first trip together to Sicily was two months after we met. We had a great year together, full of unforgettable moments that made us closer and happier every day. We both felt we wanted to do this for the rest of our lives. One day Tetiana arrived at Salvatore's house ready to spend a normal weekend together and found him in a hurry, well dressed and rather nervous. Then she noticed a beautiful huge bouquet of flowers on the bed. Her first thought was, "Is this a marriage proposal?" and of course when she turned to him, he was already on one knee and said to her in Italian, "Vuoi sposarmi?" It couldn't have been more perfect.

After the proposal we spent 5 months traveling around the world together. We went to 11 different countries in 2023! The most exciting part of the trip was to Central Asia. We are now blessed with so many great stories and memories! Once back in Vienna, we started planning our big day. We know it will be perfect because it is the day we will celebrate our love and share it with you.

Travel Information & RSVPs

By train: You can hop off either in Padua (Stazione di Padova) or in Venice (Venezia Santa Lucia). Both from Padua and Venice you will have to take a bus to continue to Chioggia. If you arrive with the train in Venice, we suggest you take a bus from the Arriva Veneto company, line 80. If you arrive in Padova, we suggest you take a bus of the BusItalia company, line “Chioggia Sottomarina Link”

By plane: Venice Marco Polo Airport is the international airport of Venice. The airport is 13 km from the center of Venice. It is named after the famous Venetian merchant and traveler Marco Polo. Travelling to and from the airport is easy, due to its vicinity to the city center and other towns. Chioggia is located about 65 km south from the airport. To arrive in Chioggia we recommend you to take a bus from the company Arriva Veneto. The bus line is number 85 and the journey takes an hour and a quarter. You can check the timetable on their website.

For those who decide to stay a little bit longer – how to get to Venice from Chioggia: Beyond the bus (Arriva Veneto, line 80), you can also choose a journey that involves a waterbus (the famous vaporetto) a normal bus and a ferryboat!
